Section 3
New — SDR Extension: Two Core Workflows

The SDR extension adds two capabilities to REV. All emails send from a dedicated sdr@kartel.ai identity, signed as the routed AE — never as AI. Every send passes through either an auto-qualification gate or a human approval gate.
